Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us

How quickly can you start my project?

We can typically start projects within 1-2 weeks of receiving your requirements and project brief. For urgent projects, we can expedite the process and begin within a few days.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients from around the world. We have experience serving businesses in Europe, North America, and across Africa. We can accommodate different time zones and communication preferences.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment terms including 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, or milestone-based payments for larger projects. We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and other international payment methods.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, bug fixes, and feature additions. We also provide training and documentation to help your team manage the solution effectively.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! We can help improve, optimize, or add features to existing applications and websites. We'll review your current solution and provide recommendations for enhancement.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We have a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our work, we'll revise it until you're satisfied. We maintain open communication throughout the project to ensure we meet your expectations.
